## What shows up in your inbox

Your inbox surfaces messages where you're mentioned, replies to your messages, and activity in conversations you're following. It groups updates by thread and conversation so you can process related items together. Think of it as your notification center—filtered down to what actually matters to you.

## Manage your inbox flow

Mark items as read to clear them from view, or archive entire threads when they're resolved. Star important messages to create a persistent reference list you can return to later. Use inbox filters to focus on specific types of updates—just mentions, just DMs, or just specific channels.

## Stay on top without staying online

Configure which conversations auto-follow (channels you join, threads you start) and which require manual opt-in. Set quiet hours so late-night messages don't interrupt focus time. Your inbox respects these boundaries while still surfacing everything when you're ready to catch up.
