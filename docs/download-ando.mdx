## Desktop apps

Ando is available for macOS Intel, macOS Apple Silicon, and Windows. Download the desktop app at [download.ando.so](https://download.ando.so). Most features match the web app, but you get native OS notifications, always-on access with dock alerts for unread messages, and keyboard shortcuts that won't conflict with your browser presets or custom shortcuts.

## Mobile apps

Ando on mobile lets you unblock work wherever you are and complements use of Ando on other clients. You can request access to the iOS and Android apps from the Ando team directly by emailing us at [hello@ando.so](mailto:hello@ando.so), or pinging one of our team members in your #feedback channel.
